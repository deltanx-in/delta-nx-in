Product

Specialists in elevator components

Elevator door sensor

An elevator door sensor is a safety device designed to prevent the doors from closing on passengers or objects. It typically uses infrared beams or pressure sensors to detect obstructions. If something is in the way, the sensor signals the elevator control system to keep the doors open or reverse their closing motion. This feature enhances safety and ensures smooth operation.

Elevator Limit Switch

An elevator limit switch is a safety device that detects the position of the elevator car and ensures it stops at designated floors. It prevents the car from traveling beyond its intended range, protecting passengers and equipment. These switches typically operate mechanically, using a lever or push-button system, and can be triggered by the car itself or by the elevator door. Proper functioning of limit switches is critical for safe elevator operation and compliance with regulations.
